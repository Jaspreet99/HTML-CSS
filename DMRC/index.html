Welcome To Delhi Metro.

Features

Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) was established by the Government of India and the Government of Delhi in March 1995 for building a new metro system in the capital. The project was carried out in phases including phase one (65.11km) and phase two (124.9km).

Increasing road traffic congestion in Delhi, which has a population of approximately 20 million, can cause overcrowding and pollution, making it an economic liability.

Why Use Delhi Metro?

Metro is easily convenient from one platform to another, from one destination to another and from one place to another. It has escalators, stairs, etc. to board and de-board the train.

You can save time by travelling from Delhi Metro. Instead of travelling by road from Delhi to Noida, which takes about 2 hours; metro can help you to reach to your destination in about 1 hour only. It saves you from huge traffic jams in the city.

Metro is quite safe for every gender. If you experience some problem, you can contact CISF personnel and metro staff that are available at each platform.
